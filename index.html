Foundation Models for the Brain

Laying the groundwork for the next stage in neurotech and AI

An invite-only research convening bringing together researchers with original approaches to data, hardware, and real-world systems.

March 26 · 5–9 PM · Brown Rudnick, New York City

Request an invite

About

This research convening brings together researchers working on foundation models for neural data and adjacent NeuroAI methods. The goal is not consensus, but clarity: what's working, what's missing, and which technical bets matter most over the next 12–24 months. The program combines short panels with substantial time for discussion and cross-pollination.
