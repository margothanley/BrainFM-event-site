Foundation Models for the Brain

Laying the groundwork for the next stage in neurotech and AI

An invite-only evening bringing together researchers with original approaches to data, hardware, and real-world systems.

March 26 · 5–9 PM · Brown Rudnick, NYC

Request an invite

About

As brain foundation models take shape, progress depends on the right people being in dialogue—across methods, sensing modalities, data infrastructure, and real-world systems. This invite-only evening convenes researchers and practitioners to compare approaches, surface disagreements, and help each other build better. Short panels open the conversation; most of the time is reserved for discussion.
